How It Works

The RGB model is an additive color model. This means that red, green, and blue light are added together in various ways to reproduce a broad array of colors. Your computer screen, phone, and TV all use this principle to display images.

  • 0 means no light (the color is "off").
  • 255 means full intensity (the color is "fully on").

When you mix these primary colors of light, you get new colors. Try it yourself with these presets!
